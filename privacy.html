Privacy Policy — Roll Along: Marble Maze

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information Roll Along: Marble Maze (“Roll Along,” “the app,” “we,” “us”) collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

We do not track you. Roll Along does not use the device advertising identifier (IDFA), does not show personalized ads, and never links your data with third-party data for advertising or shares it with data brokers.

Information we collect

Account information (Sign in with Apple). If you sign in, we receive the name and email address you choose to share through Apple and a unique account identifier. Apple may provide a private relay email that hides your real address. We use this to create and operate your account, leaderboard profile, friends, and clans.

Profile & social data. A display name you choose is shown to other players you connect with through friends, clans, and leaderboards.

Gameplay & usage data. We collect in-app activity — such as levels played, progress, purchase and ad interactions, and similar events — to run the game, power leaderboards, and understand and improve the app. These events are associated with a random app identifier and your account.

Purchases. We record your in-app purchases and entitlements so your items and lives restore correctly. Payments are processed by Apple; we never receive your payment card or financial details.

Advertising data (Google AdMob). We show ads through Google AdMob, including rewarded ads you can choose to watch for an in-game life. To serve ads, prevent fraud, and limit how often an ad is shown, AdMob collects a device identifier, coarse (approximate) location, information about ads shown, and diagnostic data. Ads are non-personalized only — AdMob is instructed not to use your data for personalized advertising.

Diagnostics. We and our service providers collect crash, performance, and technical data (such as app version, iOS version, and device model) to keep the app stable.

We do not collect precise location, your contacts, photos, microphone audio, health or fitness data, or browsing/search history.

How we use information

  • Provide and operate the app, accounts, purchases, and social features
  • Show non-personalized ads
  • Measure and improve the app
  • Keep the app secure and prevent fraud and abuse

Service providers and sharing

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and App Store purchases.
  • Google AdMob — non-personalized advertising. See Google’s Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from apps that use its services.
  • Supabase — our backend/database provider, which stores your account, social, and analytics data on our behalf.

We do not sell your personal data.

Data retention and your choices

We keep your data while your account is active. You can request access to or deletion of your data, including your account, by emailing mac.faldet@gmail.com. We will delete your account data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

Children

Roll Along is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy. We will revise the “Last updated” date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.

Contact

Questions? Email mac.faldet@gmail.com.
