## Description (4000 chars max)

```
Tilt your phone to roll the ball. Reach the rainbow.

Roll Along is a one-handed tilt-based marble game built for short
moments of zen. Hundred handcrafted levels split across two visual
worlds — World 1 takes you from clean Classic paper through Inverted,
Twilight, Ember and a climactic Aurora finale.  World 2 is Paper:
the ball leaves a graphite streak as it rolls across notebook rules,
graph paper, parchment, sketchbook grain and folded origami.

Every level has three stars to earn (clear it / clear it fast /
clear it really fast) and three coins to collect — but you can
only grab one coin per attempt, so a perfect run takes at least
three plays.  Stars and coins never expire.  Best times persist.
Replay your favourites to chase a personal best.

• 100 hand-crafted levels — no procedural filler
• 10 visual sub-themes across 2 worlds
• 300 stars + 300 coins to collect
• 6 ball skins, your name on the home screen
• Haptic feedback, sound effects, persistent best times
• Reduce Motion support — gentler physics for sensitive players
• VoiceOver labels throughout
• No accounts, no ads, no tracking, no data collection

Roll Along is built for one-handed play on the couch or the bus.
Open the app — your level is already loaded.  Tap Play.  Tilt.
That's the whole game.
```
