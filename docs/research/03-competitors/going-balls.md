# Competitor teardown — Going Balls

_Cluster: Roller (direct). Reads `02-market-frame.md`. Confidence: H=high, M=medium, L=low._

**One-liner:** Supersonic Studios' 3D obstacle-course **ball racer** — the best-monetized
*modern* roller and the closest commercial cousin to Roll Along's physics core.

## Scores (1–5)

| Core | Onbd | Reten | Meta | Econ | Monet | Viral | Social | LiveOps |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 4 | 5 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 4 | 4 | 1 | 2 |

## How it works (what's transferable)

- **Core loop (4):** roll/race a ball down a winding 3D course to the finish, dodging
  hazards; you visually **race alongside other balls** to the line. Instantly legible,
  endlessly clip-able. _(H)_
- **Cosmetic balls are the IAP backbone (Monet 4):** collectible **ball skins** are the
  headline spend — proving, in *this exact genre*, that **ball-skin cosmetics monetize**.
  Hybrid model: rewarded video + interstitials + IAP (skins / coins / remove-ads). _(M on
  specifics, H that the model is hybrid-casual standard.)_
- **Onboarding (5):** zero-friction; the loop is understood in one swipe.
- **Virality (4):** short, satisfying, "oddly satisfying" runs → strong TikTok/UA
  creatives. Growth is creative-led. _(M)_
- **Thin where Roll Along is deep:** social (1) and LiveOps (2) are minimal; retention
  rests on level cadence + skins, not events or community.

## Lessons for Roll Along

- ✅ **Borrow — racing as the legible hook.** Going Balls' core is *a race you can see*.
  Roll Along's competitive modes already do this, but the *first-session* hook sells the
  solo climb. Lead with a race. Directly supports the "see other balls" spine.
- ✅ **Borrow — ball skins are a proven roller monetizer.** Validates the cosmetics-led
  bet; Roll Along's catalogue is *deeper* than Going Balls'. The gap is visibility, not depth.
- ✅ **Borrow — clip-able runs as the UA engine.** Same lesson as Helix: build the capture/share surface.
- ⛔ **Off-limits — interstitials.** Going Balls runs them; the brief forbids them. Roll Along
  trades that revenue for goodwill and leans on cosmetics instead.
- ⭐ **Where Roll Along can beat it:** Going Balls is *shallow* (no social, no events, no
  meta depth). Roll Along already has the social backend, the content spine, and a richer
  cosmetic catalogue — i.e., the moat Going Balls lacks. Roll Along's job is to keep the
  roller juice *and* add the depth Going Balls never built.
