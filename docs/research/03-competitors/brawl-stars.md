# Competitor teardown — Brawl Stars ⭐ (the blueprint)

_Cluster: Cosmetics-led competitive. Reads `02-market-frame.md`. Confidence: H/M/L._
_This is the single most important teardown — it is Roll Along's stated stance, proven at scale._

**One-liner:** Supercell's 3v3 brawler that, after a **2024 monetization overhaul**, went from
an all-time low to **8.8× revenue** (Jun'23→Feb'24) — by becoming **more fair**, not less.

## The overhaul that matters (verified)

- **Removed gacha; de-randomized rewards.** The new **Keys + Vault** system lets players
  **choose which reward to unlock** rather than getting random pulls. **Starr Drops** are a
  *generous free* drip with guaranteed legendaries over a year. → **No loot boxes.** _(H)_
- **"Breadth over depth" pass economics.** The **Brawl Pass** is no longer buyable with the
  soft currency (Gems) — it's a real-money seasonal purchase, and the explicit goal was
  **"more people buying the pass overall, as opposed to fewer people buying more offers."** _(H)_
- **Result:** revenue **8.8×**, **MAU 2.4×**, **DAU 3.9×**. Fairness *was* the growth lever. _(H)_

## Why it monetizes (the mechanism Roll Along must copy)

1. **Cosmetics are seen by everyone, every match.** Brawlers, skins, **pins, sprays, and win
   animations** are all visible to the other 5 players in real time. Identity is *public* →
   that's the entire desire engine. **This is precisely Mac's "show opponent ball skins +
   trails" directive — Brawl Stars is the proof it works.** _(H)_
2. **A cheap, fair, cosmetic-rich seasonal pass** that the *majority* buy beats whale offers
   on both revenue *and* goodwill. _(H)_
3. **No pay-to-win:** progression is earnable; money buys *looks* and *time*, never power. _(H)_
4. **Deep LiveOps:** seasons, ranked, rotating modes, events — a reason to return daily. _(H)_

## Lessons for Roll Along (the headline borrows)

- ✅ **Borrow — the "Roll Pass": a cheap (~$5–8), fair, cosmetic-heavy seasonal pass** with a
  free + premium track. Optimize for *many buyers*, not big spenders. **This is the #1
  monetization recommendation** and is 100% inside the guardrails.
- ✅ **Borrow — de-randomized unlocks (Keys/Vault).** If Roll Along ever adds reward drops,
  let players *choose* the cosmetic — fairness as a feature, and it sidesteps the loot-box ban.
- ✅ **Borrow — public cosmetics drive everything.** Validates the spine: ball + trail visible
  to opponents. Add **win celebrations / emotes** (Brawl's pins/animations) — a new, highly
  visible, low-effort cosmetic category Roll Along lacks.
- ✅ **Borrow — fairness as marketing.** Brawl *advertised* the anti-gacha shift and won. Roll
  Along's "no loot boxes, no pay-to-win, just looks" is a genuine positioning asset — say it loud.
- ⚠️ **Adapt — LiveOps depth at solo scale.** Brawl has a team; Roll Along needs a *lightweight*
  event/season cadence (remote-config-driven) to approximate it. (Addressed in the roadmap.)
