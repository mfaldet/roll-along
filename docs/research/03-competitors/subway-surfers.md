# Competitor teardown — Subway Surfers

_Cluster: LiveOps / retention master. Reads `02-market-frame.md`. Confidence: H/M/L._

**One-liner:** The endless runner that has stayed a top-grossing/-downloaded game for **over a
decade** — almost entirely on the strength of its **LiveOps cadence.** We borrow the *engine*,
not the (ad-heavy) money model.

## The LiveOps engine (verified — this is the whole lesson)

- **World Tour — a new themed city every month.** Perpetual, themed content rotation; each
  destination brings new limited **characters + hoverboards** (= cosmetics). Never stale. _(H)_
- **Season Hunt — a tour-long collection chase.** Collect tokens across the season to earn a
  **limited character + outfit + hoverboard.** Play-to-collect, time-boxed, *aspirational not
  punitive* — a clean fair-FOMO pattern. _(H)_
- **Tiered cadence — daily / weekly / monthly events,** each a small fresh goal + reward. _(H)_
- **Community-driven.** The team **surveys sentiment each World Tour** to shape upcoming
  content — cheap, loyalty-building, and a word-of-mouth flywheel. _(H)_
- **Monetization (4):** hybrid — IAP (coins/keys/cosmetics) for spenders, **ads for
  non-spenders.** _(H)_

## Lessons for Roll Along

- ✅ **Borrow — a perpetual themed "season" cadence.** Roll Along's Challenge Tracks are
  *static evergreen*; the missing layer is a **rotating monthly/seasonal theme** that drops
  limited cosmetics and refreshes goals. This is the **antidote to the Stumble-Guys decline**
  and the home for the **Roll Pass** (Brawl) and **event skins** (Stumble).
- ✅ **Borrow — the Season-Hunt collection chase.** A fair, no-guilt event: *play through the
  season, collect tokens, earn the limited ball/trail.* Maps perfectly onto Roll Along's
  existing currency + bundle machinery — and gives tickets/coins a richer sink.
- ✅ **Borrow — tiered events (daily/weekly/monthly).** Roll Along has *only* a daily reward;
  add **weekly missions** and a **monthly season** to build the D7/D30 ladder it lacks.
- ✅ **Borrow — community-driven content (solo-friendly).** Poll players on next season's
  theme; cheap, builds loyalty, seeds organic sharing. A solo dev's superpower vs. a studio.
- ⛔ **Off-limits — the ads-for-non-spenders half** (interstitials). Roll Along forgoes that
  revenue by design; the **cadence engine** is what transfers, monetized via the fair pass + cosmetics.

## Why it's in the set

Subway Surfers is the **engine-room blueprint**: it proves that a **cheap-to-run, themed,
tiered LiveOps cadence** is what turns good content into a *decade* of retention. It's the
structural foundation under everything the Brawl/Stumble cluster does — and it's Roll Along's
**deepest current gap (axis 9 = ⚫).**
