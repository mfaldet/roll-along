# Competitor teardown — Helix Jump

_Cluster: Roller (direct). Reads `02-market-frame.md`. Confidence: H/M/L._

**One-liner:** Voodoo's 2018 spiral-tower ball-drop — the **hyper-casual virality
archetype**. Included as the *cautionary contrast*, not an aspiration.

## Scores (1–5)

| Core | Onbd | Reten | Meta | Econ | Monet | Viral | Social | LiveOps |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 4 | 5 | 2 | 1 | 1 | 3 | 5 | 1 | 1 |

## How it works

- **Core loop (4) / Onboarding (5):** guide a ball down a helix tower, smash through
  platforms, avoid the wrong colors. Understood in **under five seconds** — the purest
  "minute-one hook" in the set. _(H)_
- **Virality (5 — defining):** TikTok/social clips *were* the entire growth engine — free
  UA at massive scale; Helix Jump helped vault Voodoo to **#3 worldwide by downloads**
  (2017–18). The shareable unit = a satisfying long-combo drop **or** a rage-inducing fail. _(H)_
- **Monetization (3) — ad-only:** ~**97–99% from ads** (interstitials + rewarded + banner),
  near-zero IAP. Standard hyper-casual. _(H)_
- **Everything else ≈ 1:** no meta, no economy, no social, no LiveOps. It burned bright and
  faded — the genre's boom-and-bust signature.

## Lessons for Roll Along

- ✅ **Borrow — the 5-second hook.** Helix is instantly legible; Roll Along's onboarding
  sells a *5,000-level climb* before the player has felt the joy. Open with one
  immediately-graspable, satisfying loop (a quick race or coin-scramble), *then* reveal depth.
- ✅ **Borrow — engineer the shareable moment.** Helix's whole growth was clips. Roll Along's
  physics wins/fails are *more* expressive (cosmetics + trails + ragdoll caroms) and have
  **zero capture surface today.** This is the highest-leverage virality lesson in the set.
- ⛔ **Off-limits / cautionary — the ad-only, depth-free model.** Helix proves a thin loop
  can *acquire* enormously but **cannot retain or monetize fairly** — it has no tomorrow.
  Roll Along's deliberate inversion (depth + fairness + cosmetics) is its moat; the takeaway
  is **marry Helix's hook + shareability to real retention**, which Helix never had.

## The synthesis from the roller cluster

Going Balls + Helix prove the **front door** (legible race, satisfying clip-able runs, ball
skins that sell) — but both are **a mile wide and an inch deep.** Roll Along already owns the
depth they lack (social backend, content spine, cosmetic catalogue). **The roller cluster's
verdict: steal their front door — a legible race-first hook and a built-in share surface —
and keep our depth behind it.**
